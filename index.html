How It Works

Creating a beautiful tribute is simple with our streamlined process

1

Receive Invitation

Your funeral director creates a case and sends you secure login credentials via email to access your personalized tribute form.

2

Share Memories

Upload photos, videos, and share stories about your loved one. Our guided form makes it easy to capture what matters most.

3

Professional Production

Our team transforms your content into a beautiful video tribute with music, transitions, and professional editing.

4

Review & Deliver

Review the final tribute, request any changes, and receive your high-quality video for the service and keepsake.
